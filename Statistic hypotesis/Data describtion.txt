Data describtion

Lidar_data.csv
    1)date_started_at - date of lidar sensing.
    2)timestamp_started_at - timestamp of lidar sensing
    3)alt_start - lower boundary of HLC, detected by lidar. For cloudless atmosphere alt_start is 0.
    4)Cloud - answer for question: did lidar detected cloud or not? 1 - Yes, 0 - No


Meteo_data.csv
    1)HLC - detected or not HLC by meteorogical observation
    2)Общее количество облачности - total cloud cover
